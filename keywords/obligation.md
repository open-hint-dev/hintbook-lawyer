    What a party must do: clear mandatory language ("shall"), unambiguous as to
    who performs, what, and when. Never diluted into best-efforts or discretion
    unless the body says so. Always name the bound party (a duty with no
    identifiable bearer is reported as a gap).
    Example:
        # obligation Pay royalties
        The Licensee shall pay the Licensor the Royalty within 30 days of each
        quarter-end.
synonyms:
    - obligations
    - duty
    - covenant
