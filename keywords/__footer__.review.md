Report your review as a list of findings. For each finding include:

1. Severity — `violation` (contradicts a declaration, prohibition, or red line), `gap` (declared but missing or incomplete), or `risk` (conforms today but exposes the client against a declared position or the governing law).
2. The document section and the exact quoted language.
3. The specification block it relates to, by name or id.
4. What diverges, in one or two sentences, and the minimal revision that would restore conformance.

Order findings from most to least severe. After the list, give a verdict: either "conforms to the specification" or "does not conform", with the count of violations and gaps. If every block checks out, say so explicitly rather than inventing findings. Close by noting that the review is against the specification and is not a substitute for review by licensed counsel in the relevant jurisdiction.
