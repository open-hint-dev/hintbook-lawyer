    A narrow carve-out to the enclosing obligation, prohibition, right, or clause.
    Applies exactly as stated and no further — it never swallows the rule it
    qualifies and never reaches blocks it is not nested in. Always nest it under
    the provision it qualifies.
    Example:
        ### exception ExistingHoldings
        Passive holdings of less than 3% in listed companies are permitted
        despite the Restraint.
